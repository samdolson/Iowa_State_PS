Formula: \[\text{95% Confidence Interval} \space = (\bar{Y_1} - \bar{Y_2}) \pm t_{28,0.975} S_p \sqrt{\frac{1}{n_1} + \frac{1}{n_2}}\] Where: \[S_p = \frac{(n_1-1)S_1^2 + (n_2-1)S_2^2}{n_1+n_2-2}\]
